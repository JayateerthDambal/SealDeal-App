Seal the Deal, Faster.

SealDeal.ai is a next-generation investment analytics platform, leveraging advanced AI to automate due diligence, surface critical insights, and empower venture capitalists to make smarter, data-driven decisions in record time.

Get Started & Analyze Your First Deal

Why SealDeal.ai?

AI-Powered Due Diligence

Upload pitch decks, financials, and more. Our AI extracts key metrics, identifies inconsistencies, and performs a full SWOT analysis automatically.

Interactive Dashboards

Visualize deal performance with dynamic charts and scorecards. Get a portfolio-wide overview or dive deep into a single investment.

Comparative Analysis

Compare multiple deals side-by-side on key metrics. Instantly identify top performers and make informed decisions faster.

Agent Seal Chat

Ask natural language questions about your data. Our conversational AI agent understands your queries and fetches insights on demand.
